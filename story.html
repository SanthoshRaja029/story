FAST AND FURIOUS

        Dominic Toretto enjoys the adrenaline of street car racing and his fans treat him like a rock star.

        
        
        After a blazing encounter to take Brain,a newcomer to street racing,under his wing.Dom's sister Mia sees something she likes in Brian, too. Trouble is, neither of them realize he's an undercover cop, and Dominic and his rival Johnny Tran are both the prime suspects in a case involving dirty money and big-rig hijacking.
